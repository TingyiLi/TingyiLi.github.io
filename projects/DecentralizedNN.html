DJTable

Intro

The project is the course project from DH2413 Advanced Graphics and Interaction at KTH.

DJTable is a collaborative music experience for useless millennials who can’t actually play music. The goal of DJTable is to make a collaborative tabletop music experience for users through soothing music and synesthetic visual graphics.

Goals and Motivation

The goal of DJTable is to make a collaborative tabletop music experience for users through soothing music and synesthetic visual graphics.

Music has been proposed as psychological support for patients with low- and medium-grade depression. Therefore, the motivation of DJTable is to enable music creation even for people who cannot play any instruments. Check out the video.
